About

Who I am

I'm an experienced software engineer currently working with SkyBox Labs on the Minecraft franchise. In this role, I directed development on Minecraft for PlayStation®VR and provided assistant technical direction on Minecraft for PlayStation®4.

I have previously been employed by IUGO Mobile Entertainment, a mobile game development studio best known for creating The Walking Dead: Road to Survival and Middle-earth: Shadow of War.

I was the project manager and lead engineer of Magnesium Ninja Studios, the small independent game studio that produced Overclocked, an action-platformer for Windows and macOS.

Beyond games, I have a Bachelor of Technology in Computer Systems from the British Columbia Institute of Technology.

What I do

I am skilled in C++, C#, C, Python, and PowerShell scripting. I have experience working with the PlayStation®4 SDK, Steam® SDK, and Wwise SDK.

I am comfortable utilizing performance profilers, memory instrumentation, and crash dumps to improve product stability and overall user experience.

I have a competent understanding of both Procedural and Object-Oriented design practices, Git workflows, and CI/CD workflows. I am a vocal proponent of automated testing and effective architecting of code.
