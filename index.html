Who I am

I'm an experienced game developer and systems programmer. I specialize in high quality design, implementation, and team communication.

I'm the project manager and lead programmer of Magnesium Ninja Studios, a small independent game studio in Vancouver, Canada.

Beyond games, I will complete a diploma for Computer Systems Technology at the British Columbia Institute of Technology this Spring. I intend to pursue a Bachelors of Technology in the Fall.

What I do

I am skilled in C++, C, Java, JavaScript, web markup languages, and MySQL.

I have a strong comprehension of both Procedural and Object-Oriented design practices, as well as GitHub workflow. In addition, I have strong written and verbal communication skills.
