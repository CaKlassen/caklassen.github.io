Details

A Life Apart was developed in 48 hours for the 22nd Ludum Dare, under the theme "Alone". It is a short game about a ghost with unfinished business on Earth.

This game was my first entry into the Ludum Dare. I came 5th in the Mood category, and 75th overall.

Time Frame

A Life Apart was started on December 16th and was completed on December 19th of 2011.
