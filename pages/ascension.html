Description

Details

Ascension is a horror game about saving your daughter from a world gone horribly wrong.

Ascension was our largest project to date, taking over a year and a half to develop. It has been downloaded over 250,000 times.

We worked closely with an American musician to create a unique and chilling soundtrack.

Time Frame

We started work on the project in April of 2011 and released the game on October 31st, 2012.
