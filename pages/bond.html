Bond is a short action/puzzle platformer that I developed in 48 hours for the 30th Ludum Dare, under the theme "Connected Worlds".

Bond is about a girl and a boy from vastly different worlds working together for the first time in order to get back home.

I came 42nd overall with this game, out of 2538 entries.

Time Frame

Bond was developed from August 22nd to August 25th of 2014.
