Details

Bun-Dun was the first game we made as a team. It was developed for a month-long "invention" game jam at Game Jolt.

Our goal was to evoke feelings of reading a children's novel with the game.

Bun-Dun was developed with the help of Alexander Goncharuk, a Ukrainian musician.

Time Frame

Bun-Dun was developed in January of 2011.
