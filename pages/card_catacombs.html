Description

Details

Card Catacombs is an online dungeon crawler for 2 to 4 players. One player, selected to be the Dungeon Master, is tasked with placing traps throughout each level, making them as challenging as possible. The other players, the Heroes, must journey through each level, fighting to survive to the end of the campaign.

Card Catacombs was a joint practicum project between Rhea and I. Together, we have a significant degree of networking and game development experience, and it was our goal to challenge ourselves with creating a fun and stable online experience.

Time Frame

Card Catacombs was started in July of 2016 and was completed in April of 2017.
