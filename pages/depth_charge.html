Description

Details

Depth Charge is an eldritch bullet hell in which you must dive to the bottom of the ocean to defeat an ancient god that has flooded the Earth. Players will be challenged to both avoid oncoming bullets, while also swapping between three elements in order to destroy enemies with elemental shields. As light fades and pressure mounts, can you survive this unforgiving crusade?

I was the project manager, lead programmer, and artist for this project.

Time Frame

Depth Charge was started in September of 2016 and was completed in December of the same year.
