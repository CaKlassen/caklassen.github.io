Description

Details

Dog Yarn It was an Android app developed for Astor Tsang, a Vancouver-based knitter and entrepreneur. This project was completed in three months for an industry project course in which students worked directly with real clients.

This project also involved managing and directing a professinal graphic illustrator in order to make the app visually cohesive and appealing.

Time Frame

This application was started in September of 2014 and was completed at the end of November.
