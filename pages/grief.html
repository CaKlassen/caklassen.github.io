Description

Details

Grief was our first large-scale project as a team. It's a puzzle- platformer designed around the concept of optimism and pessimism.

A major goal of ours with Grief was to incorporate the five stages of grief into the design of the game. We wanted to discuss depression on a deeper level than just addressing it.

Grief was developed with the help of Nik Sudan, a British musician.

Time Frame

Grief was developed from February to April of 2011, taking roughly a month and a half to complete.
