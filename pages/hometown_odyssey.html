Description

Details

Hometown Odyssey is an Android application in which users can travel across their local city or town, collecting treasures and upgrading their character.

This application incorporates the use of the Google Maps API, and was developed in order to learn the ins and outs of Android development as part of an Introduction to Android Development course at the British Columbia Institute of Technology.

Time Frame

Hometown Odyssey was developed over the course of three months, starting in September of 2014 and ending in late November.
