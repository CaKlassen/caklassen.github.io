Description

Details

Minecraft for PlayStation®VR is a fully-featured port of the classic Minecraft experience, built in C++ for PSVR headsets.

On this project I served as both technical director and lead engineer. I coordinated initial technical design and general production throughout the project's lifespan, working with a variety of disciplines to complete the project on time.

This was a technically complex initiative, requiring comprehensive knowledge of the PSVR SDK and development process. In order to achieve our target framerate, I utilized a combination of GPU debugging and VR trace tooling to diagnose key performance issues and resolve them.

Time Frame

Minecraft for PlayStation®VR began production in January of 2020 and released in September of 2020.
