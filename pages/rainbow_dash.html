Details

Rainbow Dash was developed in 48 hours for a game jam hosted by the GameMaker Community. It is a simple arcade game about restoring life to the world.

My primary goal with Rainbow Dash was to develop a fun and polished experience that felt both uplifting and vibrant.

Time Frame

Rainbow Dash was developed in July of 2011.
