Description

Details

Relic Romance is a competitive hotseat game that pits two players against one another in an enclosed arena. The game revolves around a unique form of dynamic strategy and skill-based execution.

Time Frame

Relic Romance was started in the early months of 2013. It was indefinitely put on hold after the beta was released on November 9th of 2013.
