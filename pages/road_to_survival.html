The Walking Dead: Road to Survival is a strategy RPG for Android and iOS devices set in the Walking Dead universe.

On this project I worked as a C++ client software engineer, supporting with the implementation of multiple major features. I worked closely with designers and with server engineers to ensure that the features were enjoyable, and that they worked smoothly in a server-authoritative environment.

Time Frame

I joined the Road to Survival team in May of 2017 and remained on the project until February of 2018.
