Description

Details

Staged! is an action-adventure game that follows the hijinks of three oddball adventurers that have woken up in a mysterious dungeon. The player must guide these adventurers to safety, engaging in Paper Mario-like minigame combat and navigating a tile-based catacomb.

I was the project manager, lead programmer, and artist for this project.

Time Frame

Staged! was started in September of 2015 and was completed in December of the same year.
