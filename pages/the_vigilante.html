The Vigilante: Single Shot Justice was a jam game made in 72 hours for the 28th Ludum Dare under the theme "You Only Get One".

The game came 12th overall in the Jam, and was an absolute blast to make.

Time Frame

The Vigilante was developed between December 13th and 16th in 2013.
