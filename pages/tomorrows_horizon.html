Description

Details

Tomorrow's Horizon is a unique blend of strategy, adventure and emergent storytelling. You play as the captain of a crew in pursuit of the edge of the world, where you are sure you will find the answers you seek.

In order to succeed, you must manage your crew and plan ahead. Failure to do so will result in your untimely and unfortunate demise.

Time Frame

Tomorrow's Horizon is currently on hiatus due to my being in school. It was started in December of 2013 and has been developed on-and-off since then.
