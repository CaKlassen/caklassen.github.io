Description

Details

You Are What You Eat is a top-down action game in which you must navigate your way through a series of increasingly hostile levels, killing your enemies and taking over their bodies. Each enemy type has its own unique pros and cons, forcing the player to constantly adapt to their new body, and to plan out the order in which they take down their foes.

You Are What You Eat placed 9th among over 2700 entries.

Time Frame

You Are What You Eat was developed from April 15th to April 19th of 2016.
